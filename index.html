Which Learn Play Connect group are you interested in for 2022?





Learn Play Connect

This program runs for ten weeks at Adult Capability and Education Services in Random Street Reservoir.

Each session runs from 9.30 am to 12.00 pm in two blocks:

9.30 to 10.30

Parents will stay with their children in the playgroup. This will also supervised by qualified childcare workers

10.45 to 12.00

After morning tea, parents will attend a vocational pathways session. The children will be supervised by the childcare workers.

Learn Play Connect is jointly funded by Capabiity Press and the Victorian State Government Community Innovations fund
